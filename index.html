We are Flant

Being around since 2008, Flant is a Kubernetes Certified Service Provider that offers DevOps-as-a-Service and Managed Kubernetes (based on our own K8s platform).

Open Source is our strong passion and fundament that powers the company from its inception. That’s why we’re extremely happy to share our own Open Source solutions with a wider DevOps/Kubernetes community.

Here are the most noticeable projects created in Flant and available on GitHub:

  • Deckhouse А fully-featured, NoOps Kubernetes platform that works everywhere. moved to a separate GitHub organization
  • werf A powerful CI/CD tool to glue Git, Docker, container registry, your existing CI system, Helm and Kubernetes. moved to a separate GitHub organization
  • shell-operator A handy tool to easily create your Kubernetes operator using scripts (Bash, Python, etc.) or any binaries.
  • addon-operator An older brother of shell-operator extending its features with Helm charts that makes it a mighty system to manage additional components in your Kubernetes clusters in a consistent & automated way.
  • grafana-statusmap Our Grafana panel plugin to visualize status of multiple objects over time.
  • k8s-image-availability-exporter A Prometheus exporter to warn you proactively about images that are defined in Kubernetes objects but are not available in the container registry.
